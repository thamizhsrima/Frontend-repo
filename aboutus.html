ABOUT HOSPITAL

        

TVS Base Hospital is a network of healthcare in India.Founded in 2021 as a autonomas institution to serve as a nucleus for nurtuing exellence in all aspect of health care.
Twenty-five clinical departments including four super speciality centers manage practically all types of disease conditions with support from Pre- and Para- clinical departments.However, burn cases,dog-bite cases and patients suffering from infecftious diseases are not entertained in our TVS Base Hospital. We also manages a 60-beded hospital in the comprehensive Rural Health Center at Sirkali in Nagai and provides heath cover to about 2.5 lakh population through the center for Community Medicine.




OUR VISION

Our passion well reflects in the fact that we set the industry standards when it comes to cure, care and comfort.To deliver International Class healthcare with a total service focus, by creating an institution committed to the heighest standards of medical & services excellence,patient care, scientific knowledge and medical education.




OBJECTIVES

  • To develop a pattern of teaching in ungraduate and postgraduate and postgraduate medical education in all its branches so as to demonstrate high standard of medical education to all medical colleges and other allied instructions in India.

  • To attain self sufficiency in postgraduate in medical education.
