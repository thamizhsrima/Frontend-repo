A Cardiologist is a doctor who specializes in conditions involving the heart, veins, and valves. A Cardiologist can identify and treat more than 3,000 conditions. These conditions include among many others.

Cardiology is a branch of medicine dealing with disorders of the heart as well as parts of the circulatory system. The field includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease and electrophysiology.

A cardiologist is a medical specialist who can diagnose and treat diseases and conditions of the heart and blood vessels.Cardiologists work in hospitals and in private practices. They may: ... Do some procedures, such as cardiac catheterization, coronary angiography, or implantation of a cardiac electronic device such as a pacemaker. Refer you to a cardiac surgeon, interventional cardiologist, or a cardiac electrophysiologist if needed.
